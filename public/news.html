COVID-19 Update

To our dearest zoo visitors,

In these uncertain times, we value the safety of our animals. That is why we are practicing social distancing between all animals and requiring them to wear masks in the midst of COVID-19. In addition, we are using hand sanitizer on the grass to assure minimal infectious origins. As you know, current safety recommendations can change in accordance to the CDC and state guidance. We promise to keep you up to date.

We are carefully monitoring the situation with great care. If you have any questions do not hesitate to contact us. We are again grateful for your support.

Sincerely,

Mario N. Berry
Chief Executive Zoo VP, President & CEO of Strategy and Development
