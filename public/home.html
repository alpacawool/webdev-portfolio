Into the Jungle

Imagine you are the jungle. Actually, you are in the jungle. Now, imagine you see a spider crawling up your arm. It is very scary. You are scared so you leave the jungle. You never go back to the jungle again due to that traumatic event. Now imagine you are the Marionberry Zoo. Actually, you are in Marionberry Zoo. Now, imagine you see a spider crawling up your arm. It is not scary. You are not scared to leave Marionberry Zoo. You go back to Marionberry Zoo many times because you have so much fun.

Marionberry is home to over 7 unique animals and species. Founded in 2020, we are a growing at a fast pace. There are red-colored animals, blue-colored animals, and animals with noses. We also are home to the world's largest wasp conservatory. In our goal of protecting and saving endangered wildlife, we have saved over 3 wasps. We can only continue this mission with financial help from supporters like you.
